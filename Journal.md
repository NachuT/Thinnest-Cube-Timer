After this I logically put in all of the passives really close to where they needed to be for obvious reasons but then quickly ran into a sizing problem as I had components with massive packages for no reason but thats what I found first. To fix this I went into the scematic and changed a lot of compents to smd 402. FInally then it looked like everything fit until I got to the flash. The flash was such a pain because routing it normally with passives around it made the QSPI pins have 20mm differences in length which is horrible. I then deleted everything and started off with the flash which led to decoupling capacitors being a whole centimeter from their pin. I also asked for a lot of help from ppl in slack and the ee discord server and I am thankful for evryone who helped. I then once again delted and found a solution where I put the flash at a 45 degree tilt and I was just able to squeeze everything. But then I realized that my diferential pair on the USB was horrible so I also rerouted that entire section once again to get better differential pairs. From here the routing wasn't too eventful I just moved around traces and compnents as I saw fit and placed 2 ground planes which I stitched together with vias to prevent islands. I also ended up using a lot of vias for signal gpio pins throughout the board. Lastly I just added some cool silkscreen to finish the main part of the baord.
